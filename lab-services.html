📊 Lab Services

Our lab offers comprehensive data analysis services across the full spectrum of modern methodologies—from classical statistical analysis and structural equation modeling (SEM) to advanced machine learning techniques, including image processing and natural language processing (NLP).

We work with researchers who already have data and are seeking fast, high-quality analysis—without the typical delays of assigning projects to graduate students. If you need reliable results on a tight timeline, including support in writing clear methodological sections for your paper, you are welcome to contact me directly.

Our pricing is competitive, and we make the process simple, including straightforward internal budget transfers within academic institutions. Testimonials from satisfied PIs who have used our services are available upon request.
